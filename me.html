About Me

Evgeny Valeryevich Klikunov, also known as Eugene Klik, was born in Minsk, the capital of Belarus, on January 13, 1986. He has been passionate about music since childhood.

At the age of 10, he began learning piano on his own. He studied for a year at Music School No. 9 in Minsk.

He self-taught music production software like Fruity Loop Studio.

He also learned to play Casio and Yamaha synthesizers, as well as acoustic guitar.

With the rise of AI technologies, he mastered them too.

He experimented in various genres, gradually setting more complex tasks for AI, using sounds from iconic synthesizers that shaped musical history.
